Common Digestive Symptoms

Acidity / Heartburn

Burning sensation, sour belching, discomfort after meals.

Bloating

Fullness or gas after eating, swollen belly and discomfort.

Gas / Flatulence

Excessive gas, burping or passing wind frequently.

Constipation

Hard stools, difficulty passing motions or infrequent bowel movements.

IBS-like Symptoms

Alternating constipation/diarrhea, cramping abdominal pain.

Nausea / Heaviness

Feeling sick, heaviness after meals and low appetite.
